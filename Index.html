Call Flow

Power Opening Spiel with a smile😊
Be mindful with your Pace and Use Intonation Well 		“Thank you for calling your dedicated (State) Team, my name is ________. “
Long Wait Spiel (Conditional): Thank you for calling UHC, my name is ____. Am I speaking to ___? Thank you for patiently waiting on the line as we are currently receiving unusually higher volume of calls today. How can I help you?
Take a Personal Interest by Getting Their Name First and asking how they are doing today -“Who do I have the pleasure of speaking with?”
OR
“Do I have the pleasure of speaking with _______.”
AND
“How are you doing today_________”
3 PART START: Ask Clarifying Questions
Positive Answer with Temp Check: “How could I make your day even Better?”
Negative Answer with Temp Check: “I am sorry to hear that, but how could I make your day better?”

>Allow Angry Members to ‘Get It All Out’
>Understand the Member’s Emotional Drivers

Acknowledge: I understand you're calling to check if your specialist is INN
See It from the Members Perspective
Make Their Problem Your Problem
Remember the Value of an Apology

> “I realize that this situation is difficult, but let’s try and find a solution” Repeat Back and Share Their Priorities

Assurance
Positive Scripting Instead of ‘I don’t know if….’ use ‘Let me find that for you’
‘Let me make it easy for you’
‘Let me help you get the care as soon as you need’
Start Off with Something Positive “I have found the Benefit/Claim you are asking for”
“I have gotten the List of Providers in the area”
“I am getting to the bottom of this”

Don’t Leave Members Waiting. Minimize Dead Airtime and Use Positive engagement

Highlight the talking points and provide all the details connected to the member’s concern
SETTING STAGE FOR AIS
Be Respectful with the members time after providing your resolution. 		__________, could I get at least 2 minutes of your time to cover Reminders on your policy

>If Member said YES: Proceed with A4Me.
>If member said NO: Proceed with 2x48 Spiel
2x48 SPIEL I want to make sure that you view united healthcare as the best health plan possible, I would like to know if I can help you in your bills, payment, other claims, other appointments, or other questions about your account that we can assist you within 2 weeks?
RECAP | End on a High Highlight your resolution
Offering the Website provide the website details: “if you want a hassle-free experience, you may visit our website www.medicare.uhc.com and it is easier and more convenient for your end.”
BRANDING / CLOSING SPIEL >Thank you for giving your dedicated (State) team the opportunity to be the best health plan for you.

>Thank you for calling your dedicated (State) team we will always treat you with courtesy and respect, have a nice day

>Please always remember that here with your dedicated (State) team, your health is our priority and you are a family to us. Have a great day

Wrap-up Notes

-Caller is satisfied with resolution. There is no further action needed.
-Caller understands plan benefits and next steps. There is no additional assistance needed.
-Caller is aware of issue being researched for resolution. A follow up call will be made to inform caller of resolution as scheduled.
-Caller was successfully transferred to the correct department where the needs of caller will be met.
